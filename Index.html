Interactive System Guide

Learn how to navigate and use the Quote Master Dashboard

1. Overall Summary Dashboard

Displays pipeline totals, conversion rates, and negotiation/loss stats. Click on any KPI card to open a detail popup showing matching deals, and filter the Live Deal Registry below. Click the card again to reset the filter.

2. Sales Intelligence Core

Contains revenue analytics, transaction volumes, and breakdown charts. Use the Group By and Metric dropdowns inside the chart card to see revenue package-wise, client-wise, AM-wise, or source-wise!

3. QuoteMaster Form

Create new leads easily. The dropdown lists auto-complete as you type. If you are logged in as an Account Manager, the form locks to your name and only shows your mapped clients.

4. Session Persistence & Security

Logins are saved for 12 hours so you don't get logged out on page refresh. Forgot password can be used to reset access codes using a 6-digit OTP sent to your registered email.
